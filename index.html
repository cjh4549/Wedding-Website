Story

Jun and Toby met in the basement of their church while eating Panera Bread bagels. What started off as small talk over their shared interest in mental health became a close friendship. The type of friendship that laughs too hard at jokes that aren't that funny and sometimes at things that shouldn't be laughed about. These two dorks thought that their idealized INFP personalities were so similar that they were the perfect two peas in a pod.
However, they soon learned how different they really were, more like a peanut and a strawberry. Jun is the strawberry. God brought them together so that they can learn from each other and more importantly, to learn how to trust in God. Through lots of joys and hardships, they are learning how to love each other deeper every day as God loves.
Peanuts and strawberries don't go together but they can become peanut butter and jelly, making a perfect peanut butter jelly sandwich.
